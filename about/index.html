About

This blog is an experiment on using literate programming to explore Natural Language Processing and Data Science topics.

I’m César de Pablo (zdepablo) and I work as a data scientist at BBVA Data & Analytics. Before that I worked at Meaningcloud and Opinator as a developer and sometimes even went a step further ;) .

Even before that, I had an academic life as researcher in Natural Language Processing at UC3M Advanced Datbases Group

The site is built using the blogdown R package and the theme @yihui/hugo-lithium-theme and hosted in Github Pages.
